The time has finally come for that 360-degree career change. After years of working in a corporative environment I have decided to embark on a new journey. I should probably start with something like, the absolute beginner's portfolio page, nothing much to show...

Jokes aside, I am currently attending a Frontend Development Bootcamp. I have initially become interested in programming in 2018, having attended Zurich Tech Job Fair with a friend. I can thank a developer with a dog-robot who was, at the time "trying to teach a new (robot) old dog tricks". I thought to myself, oh, I want to be able to do that! So here I am, although 4 years later...

Skills I am hoping to learn in this bootcamp are:

  • Front-End foundations (HTML/CSS/Git, UI/UX Design, JS, Bootstrap, The DOM, APIs, JQuery, JSON, Ajax)
  • Front-End Frameworks (Node.js, ES6, Object-oriented programming, Test-driven development, React, Behaviour-driven development, Deployment, professional project portfolio)
  • While working on my projects: Team collaboration, Agile development, project demonstration and storytelling, understanding the bounds of reasonable and achievable)
